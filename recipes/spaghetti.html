Not Your Momma's Spaghetti

Who doesn't love spaghetti?

Not a week goes by that I don't thank the Italian culture for their massive contributions to delicious, mouth watering food! Spaghetti is one of those dishes that you just keep coming back to. The versatility of the dish allows for many individuals to make it how they see fit. Sometimes this can include igredients that others may find inconventional, but.. who cares?

For this recipe, we will be reviewing a tried and true method of making some delicious spaghetti.

Ingredients

As I mentioned, the following ingredients may not match up with everyone's personal favorite spaghetti, but it sure will be good!

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Olives, if you like
  • Whatever else it is YOU like

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta noodles per package instructions
  2. In a seperate pan, cook the meat until cooked through
  3. When the meat is cooked, pour in your Spaghetti Sauce and heat without boiling
  4. Once the noodles are complete and the sauce is warmed, you may combine them if you desire
  5. Whether combined or not, ensure your spaghetti noodles have adequate sauce, and enjoy!
  6. PS. Many people top with parmessan or Mozerella cheese for extra flavor.
