Cornbread Salad

Cornbread Salad, a delicious Southern Treat!

Cornbread Salad is a savory dish that originated in the Southern US. In areas that heavily relied on Cornbread as a staple, this meal is one that has been cooked with love for many, many years. Thanks to its' simple nature, this recipe is a favorite amongst many for potlucks, cookouts, holiday meals, or simply as a fantastic dinner side!

Ingredients

  • Mayonaisse
  • 2 Boxes Cornbread
  • 16 oz Can of Corn
  • 3 sticks of Green Onion
  • Lots of Love!

Steps

  1. Cook Cornbread per instructions
  2. Let Cornbread Cool, then crumble into large bowl
  3. Add drained corn to the bowl
  4. Chop onions and add to the bowl
  5. Add mayo (full jar, probably) to the bowl
  6. Mix well in the bowl and refigerate covered
  7. Let sit until cooled throughout
  8. Tell your Mother you love her, and enjoy the food!
